Arknights is a tower defense strategy mobile game developed by Studio Montagne under the Eagle Point Network. The game is based on a fictional story. Players will play the role of "Doctor" of the "Rhode Island" camp and place several game characters when playing levels to prevent enemies from entering the "protected target". The characters in the game are called "Operators". Players need to place operators in appropriate positions to kill enemies entering the battlefield from the "invasion point" to prevent enemies from entering the player's "protected target".
